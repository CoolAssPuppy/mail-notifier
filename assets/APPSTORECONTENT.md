## Version information

### Promotional text (170 characters max)
Never miss an important email again. Mail Notifier lives in your menu bar, quietly checking your Gmail and Outlook accounts and alerting you when new messages arrive.

### Description (4000 characters max)
Mail Notifier is a lightweight, native macOS menu bar application that monitors your Gmail and Outlook accounts for new messages and displays notifications when they arrive.

FEATURES

Multiple accounts
Connect as many Gmail and Outlook accounts as you need. Each account has its own settings for check intervals, notifications, and sounds.

Menu bar convenience
Mail Notifier lives quietly in your menu bar, showing an unread count badge so you always know when new mail arrives. Click to see message previews and jump straight to your inbox.

Smart notifications
Get native macOS notifications for new emails. Customize notification sounds per account, or set up a VIP list with special sounds for important senders.

VIP sender list
Mark important contacts as VIPs and assign them custom notification sounds. Never miss an email from your boss, clients, or loved ones.

Customizable check intervals
Set how often each account checks for new mail. Balance staying informed with conserving battery and bandwidth.

Browser choice
Choose which browser opens your email links, per account. Use Safari for personal accounts and Chrome for work.

Keyboard shortcuts
Set global keyboard shortcuts to check all accounts or quickly access your inbox from anywhere.

Launch at login
Start Mail Notifier automatically when you log in to your Mac. It runs quietly in the background until you need it.

27 notification sounds
Choose from 27 notification sounds, including classic sounds and new custom options.

Beautiful native design
Built with SwiftUI for macOS, Mail Notifier features a modern glass-effect interface that feels right at home on your Mac.

Localized in 22 languages
Available in English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Polish, Czech, Ukrainian, Turkish, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Korean, Hindi, and more.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Mail Notifier uses secure OAuth 2.0 authentication directly with Google and Microsoft. Your passwords are never stored or transmitted through our servers. The app only requests read-only access to check for new messages.

All credentials are stored securely in your Mac's Keychain, protected by macOS security features.

REQUIREMENTS

- macOS 14.6 Sonoma or later
- Gmail or Outlook account

SUPPORT

Questions or feedback? Visit our GitHub page or reach out through the app's support options.

### Notes for review
Mail Notifier is a menu bar utility that checks Gmail and Outlook accounts for new messages.

To test the app:
1. Launch the app (it will appear in your menu bar)
2. Click Settings to open the preferences window
3. Add a Google or Outlook account using the provided buttons
4. Complete OAuth authentication in your browser
5. The app will begin checking for new messages at the configured interval

The app uses official Google Gmail API and Microsoft Graph API with OAuth 2.0 authentication. Users authenticate directly with Google/Microsoft; we never see or store passwords.
